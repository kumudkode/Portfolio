Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

This privacy policy explains how Kumud Kode ("I", "me", "my") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit my portfolio website. I respect your privacy and am committed to transparency about data collection.

Information Collected

When you visit this website, the following information may be automatically collected for analytics purposes:

  • Device Information: Browser type, screen resolution, device type (mobile/desktop), operating system language
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (country, region, city, postal code)
  • Visit Information: Pages visited, time of visit, referral source (e.g., LinkedIn, Google)
  • Network Information: IP address, Internet Service Provider (ISP), timezone

How Information is Used

The collected information is used solely for:

  • Understanding visitor demographics and traffic patterns
  • Improving website content and user experience
  • Analyzing marketing campaign effectiveness
  • Identifying technical issues

Data Storage

Analytics data is stored securely in Google Sheets, which is protected by Google's enterprise-grade security. Data is retained for analytics purposes and is not sold, shared, or transferred to third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services:

  • ipapi.co: For IP-based geolocation (subject to their privacy policy)
  • Google Apps Script: For data processing and storage (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Tailwind CSS CDN: For styling
  • Google Fonts: For typography

Cookies & Local Storage

This website uses browser storage (localStorage and sessionStorage) to:

  • Remember your theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Track session information to avoid duplicate analytics entries
  • Store a visitor ID for returning visitor recognition

No advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies are used.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about data collected about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of tracking by using browser privacy features or ad blockers

Data Security

I implement reasonable security measures to protect collected data, including:

  • Token-based validation to prevent spam submissions
  • Rate limiting to prevent automated abuse
  • Bot detection to filter non-human traffic
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
